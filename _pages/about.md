I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Economics at York University, working across macroeconomics, financial economics, and development economics, with research interests in financial frictions, technology adoption, and productivity.
Before my doctoral studies, I spent over five years in applied energy economics — across the oil, gas, and petrochemical sectors — with a continuing interest in environmental and sustainability questions.
